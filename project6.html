Project 6

GPT & AI Tools Automation Suite – Real-World Use Cases

Overview:
This project showcases a suite of smart automations and tools powered by GPT-4, Google Sheets, Notion, Excel, and platforms like Make.com & Zapier. From task automation to AI-driven reporting, these tools demonstrate my ability to solve real-world problems using artificial intelligence and no-code/low-code platforms.

Use Cases Implemented:

  • Auto Email Generator: GPT + Zapier to write & send client follow-ups based on trigger words.
  • Inventory Alerts: Google Sheets + Make.com for automatic stock level updates & low inventory alerts.
  • Weekly Business Reports: GPT + Sheets API to summarize weekly sales into human-friendly reports.
  • Excel Help Assistant: GPT-powered chatbot inside Excel via custom VBA integration.
  • Notion AI Planner: Automatically builds a smart day-plan using GPT + Notion Templates.

Impact:

  • Reduced repetitive manual work by 80%
  • Saved 5+ hours per week through automated workflows
  • Improved consistency in reports and client communication

System Architecture (Visual):

Tools Used: ChatGPT (GPT-4), Notion, Google Sheets, Excel VBA, Make.com, Zapier
