Project 2

Bansal Supermarket Sales Analysis

Analysis of sales data from Bansal Supermarket to identify trends and improve decision-making.

Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from Bansal Supermarket to derive actionable insights for inventory management, pricing strategies, and sales optimization. As a GRN Officer at Bansal Supermarket, I leveraged my experience to interpret the data and provide relevant recommendations.

Key Responsibilities as GRN Officer

  • Verified floor pricing daily, increasing promotional sales.
  • Maintained high accuracy in credit/debit note entries.
  • Reduced stock-outs via real-time updates.
  • Generated daily/weekly reports (sales, inventory, rate change).
  • Coordinated with the purchasing team for inventory and pricing updates.
  • Managed all inventory and pricing tasks using R-Soft (retail software).

Insights and Recommendations

  • TIRUPATI COTTONSEED OIL 1lt is the highest-selling product but has a low profit margin, indicating a volume-driven strategy.
  • LOVE BITE NANKHATAI 500g has the highest profit margin, suggesting potential for focused marketing.
  • Consider promoting high-margin products more aggressively to improve overall profitability.
  • Monitor stock turnover rates to optimize inventory and reduce holding costs.
