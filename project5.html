Nutrition & Meal Plan

Focus on building lean muscle mass while staying within a healthy weight range of 76-78 kg. The meals are designed to support muscle growth and recovery while maintaining a balanced diet.

Protein-Packed Breakfast: Protein Oats

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 scoop whey protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 banana (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter

Instructions: Cook oats in almond milk, then mix in protein powder and top with banana and peanut butter.

Lunch: Grilled Chicken & Quinoa Salad

  • 100g grilled chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions: Grill chicken, cook quinoa, and mix all ingredients into a fresh salad with olive oil and lemon dressing.

Skincare Routine

  • Morning: Wash face with a gentle cleanser → Apply toner → Aloe Vera Gel
  • Evening: Remove makeup → Cleanse → Vitamin E oil massage
  • Weekly: Face mask with honey, lemon, and turmeric

Voice & Posture

  • Voice: Breathing exercises, humming, and deep voice training
  • Posture: Regular posture checks, neck and shoulder stretches, and core strengthening
