Nutrition & Detox

  • Start your day with lemon water + 5 soaked almonds
  • Eat seasonal fruits and avoid processed sugar
  • Stay hydrated with at least 3 liters of water daily

Healthy Recipes

  • Detox Water: Mix cucumber, lemon, and mint in 1L of water, refrigerate overnight.
  • Grilled Paneer: Marinate paneer in curd, spices, and lemon. Grill until golden.
  • Protein Smoothie: Blend banana, oats, peanut butter, and milk.

Detailed Skincare Routine

Morning Routine

  • Cleanser: Neem-based or tea tree face wash
  • Toner: Rose water spray
  • Moisturizer: Aloe vera gel or lightweight gel cream
  • Sunscreen: SPF 30+ even when indoors

Evening Routine

  • Cleanser: Mild foaming face wash
  • Treatment: Vitamin E oil massage on face and stretch marks
  • Moisturizer: Aloe vera + glycerin blend

Twice Weekly

  • Mask: Honey + lemon + turmeric for brightening
  • Steam: Herbal steam with tulsi leaves
