Project 5

AI-Powered Daily Decision System (Notion + GPT + Automation)

Overview:
This AI-driven personal system is designed to automate my daily decision-making using GPT-4, Notion, and Google Sheets. It helps me plan my day, stay consistent with habits, and reduce mental load by providing smart suggestions, reminders, and productivity prompts.

  • Auto-generates daily tasks, affirmations, and journal entries using GPT & Notion templates.
  • Tracks habits, goals, and reflections with visual dashboards in Google Sheets.
  • Uses Make.com & Zapier to sync workflows across Notion, Sheets, and email.
  • Reduces planning time by 70%, while improving consistency and mental clarity.
